Kochi Dye Shop

  • Features
  • Possible Colors
  • Ingredients

Kochi Dye Shop gives you the ability to change the color of your armor! For just 20 rupees and a few materials, you can change your armor to any of the possible colors. You can also restore your armor to the original color.

Possible Colors

Blue Red Yellow White Black
Purple Green Light Blue Navy Orange
Peach Crimson Light Yellow Brown Gray
